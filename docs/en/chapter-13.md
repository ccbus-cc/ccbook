## 13.1 Bitcoin: Digital Gold

- **Launched**: 2009
- **Consensus**: Proof of Work
- **Purpose**: Store of value, peer-to-peer cash
- **Key Features**: Maximum decentralization, robust security

## 13.2 Ethereum: World Computer

- **Launched**: 2015
- **Consensus**: Proof of Stake (post-Merge)
- **Purpose**: Smart contract platform
- **Key Features**: Largest developer ecosystem, EVM compatibility

## 13.3 Solana: High-Performance Blockchain

- **Launched**: 2020
- **Consensus**: Proof of History + Proof of Stake
- **TPS**: ~65,000 theoretical
- **Key Features**: Low latency, low fees, parallel processing

## 13.4 Cardano: Research-Driven

- **Launched**: 2017
- **Consensus**: Ouroboros PoS
- **Key Features**: Peer-reviewed research, formal verification, sustainability

## 13.5 Avalanche: Subnet Architecture

- **Launched**: 2020
- **Consensus**: Avalanche consensus
- **Key Features**: Sub-second finality, customizable subnets

## 13.6 Polkadot: Interoperability Hub

- **Launched**: 2020
- **Consensus**: Nominated Proof of Stake
- **Key Features**: Shared security, parachains, cross-chain messaging

## 13.7 Cosmos: Internet of Blockchains

- **Launched**: 2019
- **Consensus**: Tendermint BFT
- **Key Features**: IBC protocol, application-specific blockchains

## 13.8 Other Layer 1 Blockchains

- **NEAR Protocol** - Sharded PoS blockchain
- **Aptos** - Move language, parallel execution
- **Sui** - Object-centric model
- **Algorand** - Pure PoS, fast finality
